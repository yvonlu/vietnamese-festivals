Coachella Music Festival

Coachella is a world-renowned music and arts festival held in the desert landscapes of Indio, California. It's known for its diverse lineup and iconic installations.

Festival Logistics

Location: Empire Polo Club, Indio, California, United States

Dates: April 21, 2023 – April 23, 2023

Visit the official Coachella website for more information.

Lineup and Schedule

Headliners for Coachella 2023 include Bad Bunny, BLACKPINK, and Frank Ocean. Other notable performers are Calvin Harris, Gorillaz, Björk, Rosalía, Eric Prydz, Burna Boy, Kali Uchis, Porter Robinson, Blondie, Becky G, boygenius, Underworld, and Blink-182.

Check the full schedule for more details.

Tickets

Three-day general admission tickets are available in the following tiers:

  • Tier 1: $499
  • Tier 2: $549
  • Tier 3: $599
  • VIP Passes start from $1069

Tickets can be purchased from AXS, SeatGeek, StubHub, Ticket Club, TicketNetwork, TicketSmarter, and Vivid Seats.

What Reviewers Say About Coachella

Coachella, being the most profitable music festival in the US, is celebrated for its rich blend of music, art, and cultural experiences. Reviewers often highlight its evolution from a music-centric event to a comprehensive cultural phenomenon, attracting a diverse audience, from music enthusiasts to fashion-forward celebrities. However, some note that with its expansion, Coachella has also become a hub for social media influencers and big brands, which may overshadow its musical roots.

Things to Consider When Booking

  • Accommodation: Book well in advance as options near the venue fill up quickly.
  • Transportation: Plan your travel to and from the festival, considering shuttle services or car rentals.
  • Tickets: Be aware of ticket tiers and purchase early to avoid higher prices or sell-outs.
  • Weather: Prepare for desert climate - hot days and cooler nights.
